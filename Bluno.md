### 🌐 ABOUT THE ECOSYSTEM

<div align="center">
<p>The <b>Bluno Ecosystem</b> is a comprehensive supply chain network connecting wholesalers and retailers through a shared Firebase backend. To ensure maximum scalability and ease of updates, the ecosystem is split into physically <b>separate</b> client repositories tailored to their target audiences.</p>
<br/>
<p>On the B2B side, the <b>Bluno Admin & Wholesaler</b> repository handles the complex superadmin web, wholesaler app, and web dashboards. On the B2C side, the isolated <b>Bluno Retail</b> project delivers a polished, lightweight experience for retail consumers. Both platforms synchronize flawlessly using real-time Firebase listeners, GetX state management, and Hive local storage.</p>
</div>

### 🎯 WHY THIS ECOSYSTEM STANDS OUT

- 🏗️ **Decoupled Dual-Repository Architecture** — Intentionally splits the complex Wholesaler/Admin B2B logic from the consumer-facing Retailer B2C flow into distinct codebases. This prevents module bloating and allows isolated, rapid updates for either side of the marketplace.
- 🎨 **Adaptive Cross-Platform Delivery** — Simultaneously deploys the management portals to mobile devices and browsers using responsive UI approaches, while keeping the retail experience highly optimized for native mobile layouts.
- ⚡ **Reactive State & Routing** — Utilizes GetX controllers across both platforms (`cart_controller`, `auth`) for lightning-fast state updates without boilerplate.
- 📍 **Intelligent Geo-Mapping & UI** — Deeply integrates `geolocator` and `google_maps_flutter` for territory mapping, while retaining beautiful cart animations and scalable UI.
- 📊 **Dynamic Data Synchronization** — Blends `hive_flutter` for blistering-fast local cart handling with `fl_chart` for rendering wholesaler business intelligence directly from Firebase.
