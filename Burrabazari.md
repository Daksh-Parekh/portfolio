## 📱 About the App

<div align="center">

<p>
<strong>Burrabazari</strong> is a comprehensive business management application built for Indian small and medium enterprises, freelancers, and shop owners. It consolidates invoicing, legal documentation, tax filing assistance, and payment recovery into a single, elegant mobile experience — eliminating the need for multiple disconnected tools.
</p>

<br/>

<p>
What makes Burrabazari stand out is its premium dark-themed UI, a powerful custom PDF engine for generating branded invoices and legal documents, and a fully remote-configurable admin panel that lets operators manage features, pricing, and usage limits without pushing app updates. The app is live on Google Play with an integrated subscription model powering its freemium business logic.
</p>

</div>

## 🎯 Why This App Stands Out

- 🏗️ **Clean Service-Oriented Architecture** — Dedicated `models/`, `services/`, `screens/`, and `utils/` layers with singleton services (`AuthService`, `ConfigService`, `TaxServiceService`) ensuring efficient resource usage and testable separation of concerns.

- 🎨 **Premium Dark-Mode Design System** — A custom `AppColors` palette (neon cyan `#0BB3B3` on `#0A0A0A` black) with `AnimatedContainer` hover states, `Shimmer` loading skeletons, gradient overlays, and glassmorphic card styling — delivering a high-end feel.

- 📄 **Custom-Built PDF Engine** — A 1,200+ line `PdfGenerator` utility produces pixel-perfect branded invoices, rent agreements, and NOC documents with auto-calculated GST breakups (CGST/SGST/IGST), circular logo placement, HSN codes, and professional table formatting.

- ⚙️ **Remote Feature Configuration** — An `AppConfig` model backed by Firestore enables real-time toggling of features (Rent Agreement, NOC, Tax Services, Burrabazari ID) and per-tier usage limits (free vs premium) — all without publishing app updates.

- 💰 **Production-Grade Subscription System** — Full Google Play In-App Subscription flow with `syncPurchaseStatusToFirebase()` at app launch, automatic purchase restoration, Firestore premium status sync, and unique ID generation (`BURRA-XXXXXXXXX`) upon upgrade.

- 🌐 **Companion Admin Web Panel** — A dedicated Flutter Web admin dashboard with sidebar navigation, rich text editing (`flutter_quill`), tax request management, user administration, delete-account request handling, and remote privacy/terms content control.
